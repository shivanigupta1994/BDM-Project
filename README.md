# GrocerAnalytics: Kamal Grocery Store Deep Dive 🛒📊

## Project Overview
[cite_start]This is a Capstone Project for the IIT-Madras Business Data Management course[cite: 1, 2]. [cite_start]It features a comprehensive case study of "Kamal Grocery Store," a medium-sized retail business established in 2014[cite: 9]. [cite_start]The business was facing significant challenges regarding profitability, sales tracking, and inventory management[cite: 11]. 

[cite_start]The goal of this project was to extract unorganized, real-world primary data from physical bill books[cite: 13, 15], clean it, and perform a deep-dive analysis to provide actionable business recommendations.

## 🛠️ Methodology & Tools
* [cite_start]**Data Collection:** Extracted primary data for 15 key SKUs from unorganized physical records and bill books[cite: 13, 14, 15].
* [cite_start]**Data Cleaning & Analysis:** Conducted entirely in Microsoft Excel[cite: 16].
* [cite_start]**Financial Modeling:** Created a Normalized Fixed Cost Analysis and Balance Sheet[cite: 44].

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 1. Sales & Revenue
* [cite_start]The store averages a daily revenue of ₹25,846[cite: 18, 19].
* [cite_start]The Pareto Principle (80/20 rule) is highly active: Just 9 items (including Chakki Atta, Cooking Oil, and Ghee) contribute to ~80% of the total revenue[cite: 29].

### 2. Profitability Analysis
* [cite_start]**Critical Issue:** The business has a Gross Profit Ratio of only 7.94% and a severely low Net Profit Ratio of 1.91%[cite: 44].
* [cite_start]**Opportunity:** High-revenue items like Ghee (5% margin), Makhana (10%), and Cashew (6%) require immediate pricing optimization to maximize net profit[cite: 37]. 

### 3. Inventory Management
* [cite_start]Data analysis confirmed the owner's suspicion of overstocking[cite: 42]. 
* [cite_start]Items like Raw Rice, Sela Rice, Moong Dal, Cooking Oil, and Elaichi have stock levels significantly exceeding their average sales pace, tying up working capital[cite: 41].

### 4. Financial Health
* [cite_start]Despite low profit margins, the business shows an efficient Asset Turnover Ratio of 1.98[cite: 44].
* [cite_start]The Current Ratio stands at a healthy 2.40, indicating good short-term liquidity[cite: 44].
